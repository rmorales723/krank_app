ABOUT ME


Rob has been working as a personal trainer and a nutrition consultant for over a decade. As a certificated personal trainer and a successful Competitve Body Building Champion, Rob possess great knowledge of the human anatomy which is extremely important when it comes to training all types of clients. No matter what your age, gender, or body type, Rob’s passion is to train clients who want to make their dreams a reality. Rob has worked with all types of athletes, from those who are only beginners to those who want to train to compete at the professional level. As Rob has learned through his extensive experience, the gym is not only the place where you can sculpt your body, but a place where you can fulfill your dreams and change your life for the better! Having a professional trainer that is as driven, experienced, and as motivating as Rob will help you achieve every goal you set.
